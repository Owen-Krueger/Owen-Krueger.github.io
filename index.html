How Did I Get Here??

I grew up in Mora, Minnesota, on a small cattle farm. Most of my childhood was spent on the farm, and I worked for my grandfather on his farm throughout my high school career. I attended Mora High School and graduated in spring of 2015.

College of Saint Scholastica

I received my bachelors degree from the College of Saint Scholastica in May of 2019 in Computer Science/Computer Information Systems with a concentration in Software Development. During my time at CSS, I participated in the Computer Club and the Earth Action Club. I was the vice president of the Earth Action Club during my sophomore year and a co-president with another student during my junior and senior years. Also while I was at school, I worked as a computer science tutor in the on-campus Tutoring Center during my sophomore through senior years. I was the lead tutor during both my junior and senior years. On top of my tutoring job, I also worked as a tech services assistant, where I worked with the staff on campus for most of their tech/computer needs. I worked that job from spring of my sophomore year to the end of my senior year.

Life In Duluth

Living in Duluth, I was able to enjoy the many things that Duluth had to offer. I have always had a great interest in the shipping industry, and I could frequently be found hanging around Canal Park, looking at the ships coming and going through the Lift Bridge. I also enjoyed the many walking and skiing trails that populated the Duluth hills.
